La Nascita di Venere

Sandro Botticelli

1485
Italian Renaissance
Tempera on canvas
Uffizi Gallery, Florence
172,5x278,5 cm

It represents the arrival of the goddess of love and beauty
on the coast of the island of Cyprus. Rich in symbolism,
the painting represents the birth of spring and the ideal
of Renaissance beauty. The work is characterized by
a compositional harmony, with slender figures and perfect proportions. The colors are bright and delicate, with the use
of shades that create an effect of softness and depth.
