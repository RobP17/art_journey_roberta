L'Angelus

Jean-François Millet

1858-1859
Realism
Oil on canvas
Musée d'Orsay, Parigi
55x66 cm

Iconic painting of French Realism, representing a couple
of peasants praying at sunset. The scene, rich in realism
and symbolism, expresses devotion, peace and harmony.
The serene faces of the farmers, the warm light and the golden panorama create a poetic and spiritual atmosphere.
The work remains a masterpiece that moves for its beauty
and its universal message.

Timeline

The timeline outlines the publications of the artwork in various magazines over time, providing a detailed analysis of its impact on the artistic culture of the period.
On the x axis (horizontal) is represented a timeline from 1840 to 2020, while on the y axis (vertical) are listed the names of the magazines.
