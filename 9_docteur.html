Le Docteur Fautrieus

Rembrandt

1652
Dutch Baroque
Etching, tip-dry and chisel
Rijksmuseum Amsterdam, Amsterdam
20,9x16,3 cm

In the painting, the doctor is depicted with an intense
and concentrated expression, while carefully observing something outside the painting with piercing eyes and a slight hint of smile on his lips. The light that filters through
the surrounding window accentuates the details of its wrinkled face and its gnarled hands, while the chiaroscuro creates
a mysterious and evocative atmosphere. Rembrandt succeeds in capturing not only the exterior appearance of the character, but also his interior and his wisdom, making the painting
an intrinsically human and fascinating work.
