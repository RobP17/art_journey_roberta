Le Chevalier, la Mort et le Diable

Albrecht Dürer

1513
German Renaissance
Copper engraving
Staatliche Kunsthalle, Karlsruhe
24,4x18,8 cm

The work represents a knight on horseback who travels through a desert landscape, accompanied by allegorical figures of Death and the Devil. It is considered one of Dürer’s most famous works and one of the most important in the history of German Renaissance art. The composition reflects the theme
of the knight’s spiritual and moral struggle against
the temptations and adversities of life.
