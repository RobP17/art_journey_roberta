Triptyque de l'Annonciation d'Aix

Barthélemy d'Eyck

1443-1445
Netherlandish Renaissance
Mixed technique on the table
Part in the Cathedral of St. Savior, Aix-en-Provence
155x350 cm

The central panel depicts the Annunciation, with the Virgin Mary receiving the divine message from the Archangel Gabriel.
The side panels present the prophets Isaiah and Jeremiah.
The work is characterized by a meticulous realism, a rich symbolic use and an engaging religious narrative, which make
it a masterpiece of Flemish painting.
